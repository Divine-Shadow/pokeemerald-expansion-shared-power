## Shop Changes

Lilycove Department Store 4F now sells mid-tier held items instead of TMs.

| Clerk | Items |
| --- | --- |
| Left clerk | Wide Lens, Scope Lens, Shell Bell, Quick Claw, King's Rock |
| Right clerk | Muscle Band, Wise Glasses, Expert Belt, Razor Claw, Focus Band |

The Battle Frontier exchange counter no longer offers Leftovers or Choice Band. Shell Bell replaces Leftovers, and Expert Belt replaces Choice Band.

## Strict Competitive Removals

- Leftovers is no longer an S.S. Tidal hidden item.
- Battle Pyramid pickup and long-streak reward pools use Shell Bell and Expert Belt where they previously used Leftovers and Choice Band.
- The wild-held-item broker excludes Choice Band, Choice Specs, Choice Scarf, Eviolite, Focus Sash, Leftovers, Life Orb, and Assault Vest even if future species data lists one as a wild-held item.
